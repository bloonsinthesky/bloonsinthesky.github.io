About Me

Trained in management research using statistical techniques, I have extensive experience working with large data and communicating data processes and findings through scientific reports, visualizations, and presentations. My research and teaching experience spans over 10 years across Finland, New Zealand, and the U.S.

I earned my Ph.D. in Management and Strategy at The George Washington University in 2017. I also earned my Masters degree in Management at The University of Auckland in 2011 and B.A. in Economics at Amherst College in 2009.
